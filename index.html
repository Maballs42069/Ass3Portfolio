ABOUT

Leon Karssen is a Dutch illustrator known for his quirky humor and playful cartoon characters. His work features simple shapes, expressive figures, and surreal situations.


His portfolio spans fashion, skate culture, and brand collaborations, blending creativity with lighthearted storytelling.
